15 Popular Bald Black Actors You Should Follow in 2023

Publish date: 2024-09-20

Bald black actors have made a significant impact in the entertainment industry. Baldness does not necessarily mean you’ve lost your edge as an actor, and no one understands this assertion better than these actors who have embraced their baldness with confidence and have become icons in their own right. 

From the comedic genius of Damon Wayans to the versatile performances of Samuel L. Jackson, these actors have captivated audiences with their talent and charisma. Join us as we celebrate these famous black actors and their contributions to the world of acting.

    • 1. Steve Harvey

    • Full Name: Broderick Stephen Harvey
    • Date of Birth: January 17, 1957
    • Age: 66 Years Old

    Steve Harvey was born on January 17, 1957, in Welch, West Virginia, United States. As of 2023, he is 66 years old. Harvey is a highly accomplished American comedian, actor, author, and television and radio personality. He first gained fame for his observational humor and later became known for his self-help advice, particularly on relationships. 

    Harvey’s acting career began in the 1990s, and he has appeared in several films and television shows. Some of his notable filmography includes The Original Kings of Comedy (2000), Think Like a Man (2012), Think Like a Man Too (2014), and Family Feud (2010-present). 

    In addition to his successful acting career, Harvey has received numerous awards and recognition for his work. He has won multiple Daytime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Game Show Host for Family Feud. Harvey has always sported a clean-shaven head that gives him a good bald look with his signature mustache.

    2. Terry Crews

    He gained recognition for his role as Julius Rock in the sitcom Everybody Hates Chris from 2005 to 2009. Since then, he has become a prominent figure in the entertainment industry. Crews is known for his muscular physique, infectious charisma, and comedic timing.

    Terry Crews has achieved significant fame through his work in various films and television shows. He has appeared in several successful movies, including Friday After Next (2002), The Expendables film series (2010-2014), White Chicks (2004), and Deadpool 2 (2018).

Additionally, he received a BET Comedy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series in 2007.

    Aside from his acting career, Terry Crews is also known for his activism and advocacy work. He has been vocal about issues such as toxic masculinity and has shared his own experiences as a survivor of sexual assault.
