Asya Branch is an American beauty pageant titleholder who rose to fame after winning the Miss USA 2020 title in a ceremony that aired live from Elvis Presley’s Graceland estate in Memphis in Tennessee on November 9, 2020. She was the first African-American woman to be crowned Miss Mississippi USA in 2018 and she also brought the state its first Miss USA crown.
