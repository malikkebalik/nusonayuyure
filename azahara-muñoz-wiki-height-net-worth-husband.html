How did Azahara Muñoz start her Professional Career?

Azahara Muoz demonstrated her skills in September 2009, two weeks before the LPGA Tour Qualifying Tournament’s main round in California, where she placed second and moved on to the tournament’s knockout round in December.

Muoz earned $402,000 in LPGA competition during her rookie season in 2010 when she cut eleven times and finished with two top-ten performances.

She placed in the top twenty in each of the three majors she participated in in 2010, was thirty-first in terms of earnings, and came in seventeenth in terms of scoring. Muoz defeated Candie Kung 2 to 1 to win her first LPGA Tour championship at the Sybase Match Play Championship in Gladstone, New Jersey.

In the match play elimination round in 2011, Muoz lagged Morgan Pressel by two shots. In Gladstone, New Jersey, at the Sybase Match Play Championship in 2012, she achieved her first LPGA Tour victory.
