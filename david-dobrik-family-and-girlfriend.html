David Dobrik’s upbringing and family

Growing up in Vernon Hills, Illinois, was David Dobrik. When he was six years old, his parents relocated from Košice, Slovakia to that location. Toby, Sarah, and Esther Dobrik are Dobrik’s three siblings. He began playing tennis at Vernon Hills High School, where he was a student.

Subsequently, Dobrik earned a spot in the 2014 Boys Tennis State Tournament, where he finished third in the doubles division. Dobrik relocated to Los Angeles to pursue a profession as a Viner after receiving his high school diploma.

David Dorbik began as a Viner and achieved popularity on YouTube

In 2013, David Dorbik began working as a Viner. Before launching a YouTube channel, he rose to become one of the most well-known viners. He has worked on Vine videos alongside other viners like Jason Nash and Gabbie Hanna, as well as his future girlfriend Liza Koshy.

In 2015, Dobrik launched his own YouTube channel with the same name and began posting comedic videos that were based on actual events. Many of his previous Vines collaborations were also included. He started a second channel, David Dobrik Too, in August 2016 where he shares challenge videos and offers direct sponsorship arrangements.

In the meantime, Dobrik began to amass enormous fan bases as a result of his energetic videos including animals, inside jokes, and practical jokes. In December 2018, his channel hit the 10-million-subscriber mark, and YouTube awarded him a Diamond Play Button.

David Dobrik joined the Nickelodeon musical competition America’s Most Musical Family as a judge in addition to his work on YouTube. The other judges on the show were Debbie Gibson and Ciara. In the meantime, he beat out artists Shawn Mendes and Harry Styles to be crowned the Sexiest Heartthrob of 2019 by People magazine in November 2019. Dobrik hosted the American Music Awards later that month.

Relationships of David Dobrik: Is He Married?

The YouTube celebrity is single as of 2020. He is allegedly dating Natalie Noel, his assistant and best friend. Since high school, Dobrik and Natalie have been close friends. In 2018, rumors of their romance began when Natalie moved in to work as David’s full-time assistant.

Dobrik fed the rumor a few months later when he gave Natalie a car for her birthday. However, in an August 2019 interview with J-14, David denied having a romantic involvement with Noel.

David Dobrik, meantime, was previously wed to the mother of his closest buddy. In May 2019, Dobrik wed Lorraine Nash, the mother of fellow YouTuber Jason Nash, who was also his best buddy.

After Dobrik posted on his YouTube channel, the couple’s marriage seemed to be a hoax; yet, several internet publications claimed they were legally wed. But Dobrik and Nash were only able to stay together for a month or so before David filed for divorce on June 11 from Nash.

David and Liza Koshy had a devoted relationship in the past. Before they started dating, David and his ex-girlfriend Liza worked together to become successful Viners.

In November of 2015, Dobrik and Liza began dating after meeting in Los Angeles. Throughout their more than two-year relationship, the former couple was seen holding hands in several public locations.

Dobrik and Liza broke up in early 2018 after dating for more than two years, but they didn’t announce until June of the same year. In a YouTube video announcing their split, they said that the pressure of their individual YouTube careers was the reason behind their divorce.
