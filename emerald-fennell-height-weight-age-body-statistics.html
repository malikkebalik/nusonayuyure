Emerald Fennell started her career in 2006 with a role in the British TV show, Trial & Retribution. However, it would be another 4 years before she would appear in any TV show and movie. She eventually got her big breakthrough in 2011 as she was cast in the British sitcom, Chickens. She earned mainstream popularity with her masterful portrayal of nurse Patsy Mount in the BBC period drama movie, Call the Midwife. She also managed to bag the role of Camilla Parker Bowles in the historical drama web series, The Crown.
