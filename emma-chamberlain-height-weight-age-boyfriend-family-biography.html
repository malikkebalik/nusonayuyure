Emma Chamberlain is an American internet personality, YouTuber, and podcaster who has been credited as “the funniest person on YouTube” by The New York Times and has also won the 2018 Streamy Award for “Breakout Creator”. Her content on her YouTube channel has earned her a massive fan base of more than 10 million subscribers she was included in Time Magazine‘s “TIME 100 Next” list in 2019. W Magazine has also described her as “the most interesting girl on YouTube.”
