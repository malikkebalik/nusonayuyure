    Emma Mackey Facts

  • She was brought up in Sablé-sur-Sarthe and attended a Catholic school.
  • Growing up, she took a keen interest in sports and her academics as well.
  • Emma’s father is a headmaster at a school.
  • After having received her baccalauréat in 2013, Emma relocated to England to study at the University of Leeds. She was 17 years old at the time.
  • She got her big break with the show S*x Education in 2019 on which she played the main role of Maeve Wiley. Prior to that, she had appeared in various shows and films such as Summit Fever (2018) and Badger Lane (2016).

  • Emma is not much of a party popper.
