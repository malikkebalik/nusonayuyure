Professional Career of Voice Actor Eric Bauza:

Eric began his animation career as a character designer for several Hollywood production firms. As a result of his exposure to the animation community, he went on to have a successful career in voiceovers.
El Tigre: The Adventures of Manny Rivera, The Fairly OddParents, Ren & Stimpy “Adult Party Cartoon,” Coconut Fred’s Fruit Salad Island, G.I. Joe: Resolute, and Hero: 108 all featured Bauza as Red-Faced Kawn and Camel King.

He has appeared in various parts on The King and Us, a Burger King-sponsored web series, and the NFL on Fox TV pre-game show, in addition to his television credits. In Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel, he played “Digger the NASCAR Gopher.”
Bauza played Lord Stingray on Adult Swim’s Superjail!, Marvin the Martian on The Looney Tunes Show, Dr. Psychobos in Ben 10: Omniverse, and Lord Takagami, the primary antagonist in Dick Figures: The Movie. He also played Buhdeuce in Breadwinners and the title character in Netflix’s The Adventures of Puss in Boots.
