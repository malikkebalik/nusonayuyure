Theresa Kufuor: A Key Figure in Ghana Passes Away

The news of Theresa Kufuor’s passing has left the nation of Ghana mourning the loss of a key figure. As the former First Lady and wife of the second President of the Fourth Republic of Ghana, John Agyekum Kufuor, Theresa Kufuor played a significant role in shaping the country’s history. Her contributions to Ghana will always be remembered and cherished.

Theresa Kufuor was born on October 25, 1935, and passed away at the age of 88 on October 1, 2023. She had just celebrated her 61st wedding anniversary with John Agyekum Kufuor on September 8, 2023. Their love story is one that has captured the hearts of many Ghanaians.

Throughout her life, Theresa Kufuor was dedicated to serving her country and its people. She championed causes such as reducing maternal mortality rates and promoting access to quality education for all children.

They first met at a Republic Day dance in London in 1961, where they struck up a conversation and quickly became friends. John, who had just completed his law studies, was immediately drawn to Theresa’s intelligence and charm. Meanwhile, Theresa was impressed by John’s ambition and dedication to his studies.

After a year of friendship, John mustered up the courage to ask Theresa to be his wife. They tied the knot on September 8, 1962, marking the beginning of their lifelong commitment to each other. Over the years, their love only grew stronger as they navigated the ups and downs of life together.

In their 61 years of marriage, Theresa and John were blessed with two children. Their first child was born in 1963, followed by their second child in 1964. Throughout their journey, they prioritized their family and worked hand-in-hand to create a loving and supportive home environment.
