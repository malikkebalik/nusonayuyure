Francesca Hung Bio | Journey To Miss Universe 2018

Francesca Hung is the winner of Miss Universe Australia 2018 competition. The Northern Sydney native works a full-time model for Westfield and Slate Swimwear. However, the road to modeling industry wasn’t easy for her. Speaking with News.com.Au, she said:

 ‘I always wanted to get into modelling and media, but I really struggled to break in to an agency. Eventually someone [Scoop Management] snatched me up.’

Fulfilling her dreams, Hung eventually became Miss Universe Australia 2018. At present, she is giving her best to bring Miss Universe crown home.
