Who is J. Alphonse Nicholson?

J. Alphonse Nicholson is an American well-named actor and percussionist. He is best known for his notable works including P-Valley, Just Mercy, Mr. Robert Season 2, and Chicago PD.

He is currently a brand ambassador of Ball Family Farms.

J. Alphonse Nicholson: Age, Family, Siblings, Ethnicity

The actor J. Alphonse Nicholson was born on July 6, 1985, in Greensboro, North Carolina. At present, he is 35 years old and his Zodiac sign is Cancer.

He holds an American nationality and belongs to the Afro-American ethnicity.

He hasn’t yet disclosed his parent’s details to the media. However, he has a sister named Sade Nicholson.

In which school did J. Alphonse Nicholson attend?

Talking about his academics, J. Alphonse Nicholson enrolled at James B. Dudley High School.

For further studies, he attended North Carolina Central University and majored in theater. However, he later dropped out of school and then started pursuing his career.

J. Alphonse Nicholson: Professional Life, Career

Prior, J. Alphonse Nicholson was a street drummer and one of his director friends saw his video and allowed him to compose a show at the Lincoln Center Education program.

Through his street journey, he landed in the acting field and he made his first role in Howard L. Craft’s show, “Caleb Calypso and the Midnight Marauders”.

Afterward, he got a breakthrough with his appearance in the TV series, P-Valley where he portrayed the role of Lil Murda.

Similarly, J. Alphonse Nicholson has appeared in several broadways like Roundabout’s “A Soldiers Play”, OFF-BROADWAY: Signature Theatre’s PARADISE BLUE (Blue), 2nd Stage “Days of Rage” and “FREIGHT” (NFT).

In 2016, he made an appearance in an episode of Marvel’s Netflix television series, Luke Cage where he portrayed the role of Darnell.

J. Alphonse Nicholson starred in the 2019 biographical legal drama film, Just Mercy as Henry Davis. He starred along with Michael B. Jordan, Jamie Foxx, Rob Morgan, and more.

Lately, he was cast in the role of John Robinson in the 2020 Netflix miniseries, Self Made. The series is based on the biography of A’Lelia Bundles’s On Her Ground.

Furthermore, some of J. Alphonse Nicholson’s notable films and television series are Blue Bloods (2010), Shots Fired (2017), The Backlist (2013), Tales(2017), and more.
