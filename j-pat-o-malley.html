Biography

J Pat O’Malley was born in England on March 15, 1904. Remembered for his memorable guest star roles on such American television series as The Rebel, Black Saddle, and Perry Mason, this English-born performer also frequently worked as a voice actor, contributing to such animated films as Alice in Wonderland and The Jungle Book. He appeared with Gene Kelly in several episodes of the 1960s ABC series Going My Way. Before pursuing a screen acting career in the United States, he sang and recorded popular songs with Jack Hylton’s United Kingdom-based orchestra.
