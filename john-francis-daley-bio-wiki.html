John Francis Daley Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Who is John Francis Daley?

John Francis Daley is an actor and a musician. Additionally, he is also a director, screenwriter, and producer. In 2014, he earned the nomination for a PRISM Award.

Presently, he is a prominent figure among the media for appearing in NBC’s TV series, “Freaks and Geeks”. He played the character of Sam Weir in the series.

Additionally, he is also famous for starring as FBI criminal profiler Dr. Lance Sweets on the series “Bones”.

What are the birth facts of John Francis Daley?

John was born in the city of Wheelings in the state of Illinois. Born on July 20, 1985. He possesses American nationality and his ethnicity is mixed(Irish and Ashkenazi Jewish).

He was born to his parents, R.F. Daley (father), and Nancy Daley (mother). His father is an actor and his mother is a piano teacher. Although he was born in Wheeling, he was raised in Nyack, New York by his parents.

John Francis Daley: Education History

As per his education, he attended Nyack Middle School in New York. He was involved in acting since his middle school days.

John Francis Daley: Professional Life and Career

John initiated his acting career at the early age of 13 or 14. He played the character of Danny in his middle school’s musical, “Grease”.

He officially started his acting career in the U.S. and international tours of the Broadway play “The Who’s Tommy”. John played the character of Young Tommy in the play. Afterward, he joined the cast of “Freaks and Geeks” in 1999.

He became famous after the portrayal of Sam Weir in the short-lived series. Although the series had a short-lived career of 1 year, it made him popular and raised his fame.

Contrary to TV, he made his debut in a feature film in 2003 film, “View from the Top”. The same year he also played the character of ‘Grant Binder’ in the TV series, “Regular Joe”.

His fame reached the top level after he starred as Dr. Lance Sweets in the TV series, “Bones”. He featured in 138 episodes of the show from 2007 to 2014.

Additionally, he starred in movies like, “Waiting”, “Rapture-Palooza”, and others. Similarly, he featured in several TV projects like, “Boston Public”, “Kitchen Confidential” and so on.

Multi-talented John is also a writer and has co-written films like, “Horrible Bosses I & II”, “Spiderman-Homecoming”, and others.

As a director, he has co-directed films like, “Audio Tour”, “What Babies Do”, etc. Also, he plays keyboards and sings for the band, “Dayplayer”.
