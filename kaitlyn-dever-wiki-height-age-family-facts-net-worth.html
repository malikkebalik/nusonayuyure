Kaitlyn Dever is an American actress, Instagram star, and social media influencer. She is popular for her movies and Tv shows such as Bad Teacher, Beautiful Boy, Booksmart, Private Practice, and Unbelievable. She is also known for appearing in a music video “Graduation” by Benny Blanco & Juice Wrld.

Career 

Kaitlyn began her acting career as Gwen Thompson in a 2009 show titled “An American Girl: Chrissa Stands Strong”. However, her role in the show was limited, but it impressed the audience and critics. In the same year, she acted in another show called “Make It or Break It”. Kaitlyn gained popularity as an actress in 2011 because of her fabulous role as Loretta McCready in a crime drama show “Justified”. 

With rising popularity as an actress, Kaitlyn appeared in some successful and popular shows including The Mentalist, Curb Your Enthusiasm, Last Man Standing, Unbelievable, Monsterland, and more. In 2011, she made her movie debut as Sasha Abernathy in the comedy movie “Bad Teacher”. Some popular movies of Kaitlyn are The Spectacular Now, Men, Women & Children, The Front Runner, Them That Follow, Dear Evan Hansen, and more. 

In 2016, Kaitlyn gave voice and motion capture to Cassie Drake, a popular character of Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End game. She had also given voice for the Open Roads game. Besides this, she has appeared in several music videos including “Graduation” of Benny Blanco & Juice Wrld. 

Awards and Achievements

In 2011, Kaitlyn received a nomination for the Young Artist Award for her fabulous performance in Private Practice. In 2013, she also received a nomination for the Phoenix Film Critics Society Award for her popular movie “Short Term 12”. 

In 2019, Kaitlyn won the Newport Beach Film Festival Award under the Ensemble Cast category for Them That Follow. In the same year, she won the CinemaCon Award in the Female Stars of Tomorrow category. 

In 2020, Kaitlyn received a Golden Globe Award nomination Golden in Globe Award for Best Actress category. In the same year, she won a nomination in the British Academy Film Award for the Rising Star Award category.
