Publish date: 2024-08-28

The Griffin-Spalding County community is grieving the loss of a 14-year-old student, Emmanuel Dorsey, who tragically lost his life in a shooting incident over the weekend. The suspect, identified as Kaomarion Kendricks, was also a student at Griffin High School, and this heartbreaking event has left the community shocked and saddened.

“Griffin Georgia Police Searching 17-Year-Old Kaomarion Kendricks, Who They Say Is Responsible For Shooting And Killing A 14-Year-Old Outside Of A Football Game Saturday Afternoon – Atlanta Georgia reporting
– According to Griffin Police and the Spalding County Sheriff’s Office, Kendricks knew the 14-year-old he killed.
They found the 14-year-old victim in the street.
▶︎ Kendricks is facing outstanding warrants for charges including murder, aggravated assault, minor in possession of a firearm, possession of that firearm during the commission of a felony, and reckless conduct.
✔︎ “It’s just honestly scary to think about. And I think gun laws just be a little stricter, especially with them getting into minor’s hands. It is very, very scary to think about,” neighbor Naulls said.”

The shooting occurred near Poplar St. and 5th St. on Saturday afternoon, close to the end of a football game. It was clarified that this was not a random shooting, as both the victim and the suspect knew each other. The incident did not take place within the football stadium, and Kendricks did not attend the football game.

In response to this tragic event, the Griffin-Spalding County School System has announced increased security measures in the high schools. The district emphasized their commitment to keeping school environments safe by utilizing weapons detection systems and having school resource officers in place.

Counselors and mental health clinicians will be available to provide support to students and staff in both high schools during this difficult time. The community is urged to come forward with any information related to the incident, and Kendricks is considered armed and dangerous.

This senseless act of violence has deeply impacted the community, and thoughts and prayers are extended to the family and friends of Emmanuel Dorsey during this heartbreaking time. The district emphasizes a zero-tolerance policy for violence, reiterating their dedication to ensuring the safety and security of their schools.
