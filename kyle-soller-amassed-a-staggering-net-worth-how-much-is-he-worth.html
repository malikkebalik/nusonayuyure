Kyle Soller Amassed a Staggering Net Worth; How Much is He Worth?

Publish date: 2024-09-07

If you follow the British-American entertainment industry, then Kyle Soller is no new name. He is no stranger to stardom and glory since he is an active performer in movies, television, and theatre.  

Soller is actively working in the glamor industry since 2008 and never looked back ever since. Thus, today, we focus intensely on his professional journey to stardom. 

Staggering Net Worth of $1 Million

The multiple-award winner Kyle Soller makes a significant worth of $1 million as of 2019. Undoubtedly, the primary credit goes to his ambitious and sound acting profession.

Kyle is most famous for his performances in Marrowbone and The Inheritance. 

Award-Winner in Broadway

Soller enjoyed most of his success when working for the theatres. He bagged the Oliver Award for Best Actor in the theatrical performance of The Inheritance in 2018. 

Earlier in 2011, he won the Evening Standard Award for Outstanding Newcomer in The Government Inspector as Khlestakov. Furthermore, he was also nominated in the WhatsOnStage Award and Oliver Award for a few plays.  

Eight Big-Screen Projects 

The 36-year-old appeared in different roles in a few filmography projects like The Fifth Estate and Anna Karenina. Moreover, The Titan (2018) falls on his latest project. The movie grossed only $2.6 million at the box office.

One of Kyle’s best work came in 2017 as Marrowbone. He played Porter in the classic horror picture and earned a warm personal response with his performance. The movie made significant profits after accumulating $11.2 million in revenues worldwide.

The actor featured as Jonathan in The Trip to Spain in 2017. Despite his minor role, fans loved his portrayal in the show. In fact, the movie amassed $2 million in its revenues.

According to the renowned critique, Rotten Tomatoes, Kyle’s highest-rated project came in as The Fury (2014) starring Brad Pitt, Shia LaBeouf, Scott Eastwood, and such.

Multiple Credits on Television

The elegant personality made his television debut in an episode of BBC‘s Bad Education. Furthermore, he appeared in fourteen episodes of the British show, Poldark, as Francis Poldark.

Continuing his successful journey, he landed the role of Zack Massingham in HBO’s Brexit. He shared the screen alongside the reputed Benedict Cumberbatch and Lee Boardman.

Blissfully Married to Phoebe Fox

The 36-year-old Kyle Soller is happily married to Phoebe Fox for about a decade now. The funny thing about destiny, the two met the first time at RADA. The adorable couple dated sometime before exchanging the vows in 2009.

As of now, the artist is living with his wife in London with two cats. The fans are increasingly excited over the prospect of a future family of the celebrity couple.
