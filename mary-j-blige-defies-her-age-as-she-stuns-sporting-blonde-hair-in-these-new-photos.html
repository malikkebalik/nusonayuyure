Mary J Blige Defies Her Age as She Stuns Sporting Blonde Hair in These New Photos

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Popular singer Mary J Blige shared new pictures of herself looking youthful with a blonde hairstyle, and fans could not stop swooning over her good looks.

Mary J Blige shared new photos on her Instagram, and from all indications, the multiple award-winning singer and actress is indeed a timeless beauty.

In a picture posted on her Instagram feed, Blige was seen donning a gorgeous red sweater. She rocked the top alongside a gold Cuban necklace and a tennis necklace with a large pendant.

Mary J. Blige at the AFI FEST 2017 on November 9, 2017. | Photo: Getty Images

Blige topped off her look with subtle make-up and wavy blonde hair complementing her skin tone. The singer showed off her gorgeous hairstyle by letting it fall over her shoulders.

With her head tilted to the side, she stared intently, sporting a fierce look while posing for the beautiful selfie. She accompanied it with a caption that read:

"I thought somebody said something."

As expected, some of the singer's four million-plus followers were thrilled by the picture and soon flocked her comments section with lovely compliments.

Blige further showed off her gorgeous look on her Instagram stories. While the first picture was similar to the one on her feed, the second one showed her wearing a red fur coat over her sweater.

She showed up at the Oscars wearing a lovely white Versace dress paired with red-bottomed heels.

The singer's last picture saw her sitting on a chair in what appeared to be a make-up and hair studio. With one finger held against her head, Blige posed for the gorgeous selfie.

While Blige is best known for her career as an actress and a singer, the 49-year-old has made a name for herself as a fashion icon and trendsetter in her own right.

A selfie of Mary J. Blige flaunting her blonde hair wearing a red fur coat. | Photo: Instagram/Therealmaryjblige

Earlier this year, Blige shared a video of herself rocking a long-sleeved, attention-catching sparkly dress while enjoying a night out on New Year's Eve.

The singer accessorized with some gold earrings, rings, and a pair of sparkly heels. Blige accompanied the clip with a caption wishing her followers a happy new year.

A selfie of Mary J. Blige flaunting her blonde hair and her gold chains. | Photo: Instagram/Therealmaryjblige

Another remarkable fashion moment for Blige was in 2018 when she showed up at the Oscars wearing a lovely white Versace dress paired with red-bottomed heels.

With two major nominations, it was only fitting that Blige would show up like the beautiful Black goddess that she has constantly proven herself to be.

Not many people can pull off being a successful actress and singer, but for Blige, this is only one of the numerous things she can do. Now, who would not love this Black queen?
