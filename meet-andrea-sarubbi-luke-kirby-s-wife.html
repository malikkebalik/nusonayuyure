Luke Kirby and Andrea Sarubbi’s Marriage

After divorcing his ex-wife, Luke started dating costume designer Andrea Sarubbi. For a while, they were the talk of the town, but when they both kept quiet about it, the rumors stopped. Following all the connections, a further key allegation—that he had an affair with Tom Cruise’s ex-wife, actress Katie Holmes—came to light.

Kirby and Sarubbi have been dating for a long time. The couple has been together for a very long period, as evidenced by ten-year-old stories that described them as “long-term” lovers.

She was a wardrobe stylist for the 2000 television documentary Treasure Seekers, according to IMDb. Her professional whereabouts remain unknown today, nevertheless.
