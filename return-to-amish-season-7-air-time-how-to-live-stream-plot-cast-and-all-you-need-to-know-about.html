'Return to Amish' Season 7: Air time, how to live stream, plot, cast and all you need to know about

Publish date: 2024-09-09

If you have always been curious about the Amish lifestyle and culture then TLC's popular show 'Return to Amish' should definitely be on your watchlist. The hit reality show features a group of Amish people trying to break away from their community and exploring the outside world which they refer to as the "English world".

Over the span of six seasons, we have seen several Amish followers trying to make sense of the English world and making their big decision on whether they want to return to their old conservative lifestyle or remain in the English world and explore new opportunities presented to them. Season 6 of 'Return to Amish' left fans feeling super excited and thrilled after watching the transformation of the new cast members -- Maureen and Rosanna -- into English girls ready to embark on the new lifestyle. Although TLC hasn't made any official announcement yet about 'Return to Amish' Season 7, it's hard to imagine the network canceling one of their most popular shows. While we eagerly await any updates on the new season, here's all we know about 'Return to Amish' Season 7.

Air time

TLC is yet to make an announcement about the renewal or cancelation of 'Return to Amish' Season 7. Although there is absolutely no reason for the network to not renew the hit show for another season. So keep watching this space for further updates on the show.

How to live stream

You can catch up on the old episodes of 'Return to Amish' while waiting for the news of the new season on TLC. There are several other ways that one can tune into the show. For viewers who wish to watch the show without a cable connection, Philo, fuboTV, Sling TV, Hulu with Live TV, AT&T TV and YouTube TV seem to be the best options available. 

Apart from this, Discovery Plus subscribers can catch the episodes anytime anywhere on the app. Episodes are also available to view on TLC's website, Amazon Fire TV, Roku and Apple TV. Viewers can also download the TLC Go app to tune in to the episodes.

Plot

Season 6 of 'Return to Amish' featured the return of Jeremiah Raber and his wife, Carmella Raber. Jeremiah finally got to learn more about his biological parents after tracing the roots of his biological family. Although he was shocked and upset to learn about the tragic murder of his father, the reality star was still glad to have finally found his family and feel belonged. With his newfound perspective towards life, he was shown expressing his desire to have children with Carmella, To his surprise, Carmella was more than happy to start a family with him. So hopefully we will get to see more on that in the upcoming season of 'Return to Amish'.

Sabrina Burkholder and Jethro Nolt continued to struggle with their relationship, but the addition of another child into their family finally seems to have helped Jethro prioritize his family. Despite their many issues, the couple is more determined than ever to make things work for the sake of their children. Hopefully, things would have got better between Sabrina and Jethro by the time next season premieres.

Maureen and Rosanna, the newest additions to the cast of Season 6 managed to steal the show with their innocence and curiosity towards English life. The girls decided to turn English and had their big transformations during the season finale. Maureen got engaged to her boyfriend Daniel after her transformation, while Rosanna is determined to continue living freely and enjoy her independence. We can't wait to see more of Maureen and Rosanna on Season 7.

Cast

Season 6 of 'Return to Amish' featured Jeremiah Raber and wife Carmella Raber, alongside Sabrina Burkholder and her boyfriend, Jethro Nolt. Maureen and Rosanna were the new additions, and Maureen's grandmother Ada continued to guest star on the show. We will have to wait for updates from TLC to see if there are any new changes to the present cast.

Trailer

There's no trailer for season 7 of 'Return to Amish' yet. Keep watching this space for further updates.
