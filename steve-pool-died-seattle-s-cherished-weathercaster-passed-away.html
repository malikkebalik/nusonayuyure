Steve Pool Died : Seattles Cherished Weathercaster passed Away

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Steve Pool, the beloved weathercaster from KOMO, has sadly passed away. Known for his infectious enthusiasm and unrivaled commitment to meteorology, Pool leaves behind a legacy that will continue to influence the world of broadcasting.

The Man Behind the Forecast

Born on November 5, 1953, Steve Pool was more than just a weathercaster; he was an integral part of the community. For decades, Seattle residents relied on his forecasts to navigate their days. His ability to simplify complex weather patterns into easily digestible information earned him a special place in the hearts of viewers.

A Pioneer in Broadcasting

During his tenure at KOMO 4, Pool became a pioneer in Seattle broadcasting. His warm presence and professional approach set a high standard for weather reporting. He was not just a meteorologist delivering the forecast but a storyteller who made weather an engaging narrative.

The Battle with Illness

Steve Pool courageously fought against early onset Alzheimer’s disease. His battle with the illness was both brave and inspiring, demonstrating his resilience in the face of adversity.

Dear Friends,I am here to share the sad news that my dear husband, my love, has passed away from early-onset Alzheimer’s disease. He fought this terrible disease privately for several years, and with every ounce of his being. He told me multiple times to “never count me out” and we never did. This past week it became too much and he passed away peacefully. We are so blessed to have had him in our lives. He was an extraordinary man, husband, father and good friend to many. Please know that he truly loved his job and this community and felt so privileged to be a part of your lives. You were all so good to him and thereby good to us. Our hearts are irretrievably broken. Please say a prayer for him and our family. Much love, Michelle and our daughters Lindsey and MarissaPicture credit: Jennifer Winter Photography

Remembering Steve Pool

Steve Pool’s passing is a significant loss for the community of Seattle and the world of broadcasting. His influence reached far beyond the screen, touching the lives of many. As we mourn his untimely demise, we also celebrate the life he lived and the significant impact he made in his field.

His memory will live on in the accurate forecasts, the engaging stories, and the warm smiles he shared with his audience. Steve Pool may have left us, but his legacy remains, reminding us of the man who brought sunshine to our screens, regardless of the weather outside.
