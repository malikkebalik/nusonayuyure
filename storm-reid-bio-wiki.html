Storm Reid Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Who is Storm Reid?

Storm Reid is an American actress who is known for the leading role of Meg Murry in the film ‘A Wrinkle in Time’ in 2018.

She is also famous for her role as Emily in the Oscar-nominated movie ‘12 Years a Slave’ in 2013. She has been able to win millions of hearts through her performance.

How old is Storm Reid?

She was born on July 1, 2003, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. She is currently 18 years old.

Her father’s name is Rodney Reid and her mother’s name is Robin Reid. She has got a sister but her name is not disclosed yet.

Storm Reid: Education, School/College University

Regarding her education history, she is a graduate student but she has not disclosed her college name.

Storm Reid: Professional Life and Career

She started acting at the age of 3. She played small roles as Greta in the short film ‘The Girls of Summer’ in 2012 and a little girl in ‘The Spirit Game’ in 2013.

After that, she made her breakthrough in her role as Emily in the Academy Award-winning film ’12 Years a Slave’.

Then, she appeared as Kendra in the film ‘The Summoning’ in 2015 and landed a major role as Tina Wolfe, younger sister of the film’s protagonist Bo, in the movie ‘Sleight’ in 2017.

She portrayed the role of Meg Murry in the film ‘The Wrinkle in Time’ in 2018, Ashley in ‘Relive’ in 2019, and Abigail in ‘Killing Winston Jones in 2019.

Her recent television roles include Scarlett in ‘Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn’ in 2014, Denise in ‘Chicago P.D.’ in 2015, and also made her appearance in ‘Euphoria’ as the main role in 2019.

Storm Reid: Awards, Nominations

She was nominated for Teen Choice Award in the category Choice Movie Actress: Fantasy for ‘A Wrinkle in Time’ in 2018.
