The Ace Family’s net worth, biography, and recent updates…

Austin McBroom and Catherine Paiz established the renowned American YouTube channel, the Ace Family. The channel is famous for sharing reaction videos, pranks, challenges, vlogs, and comedy content, boasting around 18 million subscribers and over 340 million views. In addition to their YouTube presence, the Ace Family has a vast following on various social media platforms, with their Instagram account amassing over 14.2 million followers.

The Ace Family: Early Background

Austin McKinley McBroom

Austin, born on May 20, 1992, in Palmdale, North Hollywood, California, ventured into soccer and basketball during his childhood. His father, Allen McBroom, and mother, Michole McBroom, both made appearances on his YouTube channel, despite being divorced. Austin attended Campbell Hall School for his high school education and later pursued his bachelor’s degree at Saint Louis University in Montreal, Canada.

Dolores Catherine Johnston McBroom

Catherine, born on August 24, 1990, in Montreal, Canada, grew up in a middle-class family and harbored a passion for acting and games from an early age. Her father, Mr. Johnston Paiz, owns a grocery store, while her mother, Mrs. Dolores Catherine, works as a banker. Similar to Austin’s channel, Catherine’s parents have shown support by featuring in her content. She completed her high school and graduation in Montreal, Canada.

The Ace Family: Personal Life

Austin and Catherine met in 2015 at the University, eventually getting engaged in 2017 and tying the knot the same year. The couple has three children together: Elle Lively McBroom, Alaia Marie McBroom, and Steel McBroom.
