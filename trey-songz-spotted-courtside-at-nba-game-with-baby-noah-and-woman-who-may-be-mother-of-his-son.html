Trey Songz Spotted Courtside at NBA Game with Baby Noah and Woman Who May Be Mother of His Son

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Famous American singer Trey Songz attended the recently-held NBA game with his little son, Noah, but all attention was on the mystery woman who was spotted sitting courtside with them.

Being a father sure looks good on the famous singer as he cuddled to his adorable son, who was born April 20 and is thus only a few months old. However, it was hard to miss that a gorgeous woman accompanied the father-son duo.

Trey Songz at the MCM x Bape VIP Collection Launch at No Name on October 24, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

The Shade Room sparked speculations on Instagram when they shared pictures of Trey and the woman alongside the obvious question of her identity. The caption of the three snaps read:

"Oop!! Harpo, who is this woman that was spotted having a good time with #TreySongz and his baby boy at yesterday's Knicks game?"

The comments section of the post is lit up with similar questions from netizens as they equally expressed curiosity at the woman's identity.

Now, reports share that the stunning woman, who has been identified as New York native, Caro Colon, is the mother of Trey's only child, which explains her presence at the recent outing.

Caro, who looked very much like baby Noah, sat front and center wearing a green cap and a brown coat. The new mother was spotted taking several pictures of her son at the NY Knicks game while sitting by the courtside alongside her alleged baby daddy.

There is no arguing that the trio made a stunning group, but what is much more important is the affectionate air between the parents as they doted on Noah.

It was only a few months ago that Trey Songz revealed that he had become a father, and ever since, he has shown himself to be the proud daddy by posting several snuggly photos of himself and the sweet youngster.

Late last month, the "Can't Help but Wait" crooner took to Instagram to mark Noah's sixth month with a series of beautiful snaps and clips that left fans awestruck.

"Six months ago, today, you changed my life. Words can't describe."

Thanks to these affectionate displays, Trey is fast becoming a fan favorite online with most of the posts amassing over 1 million likes as well as thousands of comments each!

This is more proof that the internet really does love celebrity babies!
