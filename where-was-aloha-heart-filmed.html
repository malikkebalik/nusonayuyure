Where Was Aloha Heart Filmed?

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Aloha Heart was filmed in Honolulu, Waimanalo, and Makapu'u in Oahu, Hawaii. The production began on April 22, 2023.

The movie was shot on the island of Oahu called Honolulu.

The Manu hotel featured on the show is The Kahala Resort which they renamed 'Hale HoAloha Resort.'

Besides this, they also spotted shooting on the island of Oʻahu, Hawaii, including Waimanalo and Makapu'u. 

The production team started shooting on April 22, 2023 and wrapped up on May 22, 2023.

The day before Hallmark's upcoming film production, actress Olivia Nicole Hoffman shared she is heading to Hawaii to shoot her next Hallmark film.

The actress conveyed the clips and pictures while enjoying her time in Hawaii. Besides shooting, the cast member takes some free time to explore the beautiful destination.

On May 28, 2023, Olivia captioned, 'Farewell Hawaii.. look out for Aloha Heart premiering on Hallmark channel soon.'

Besides her, other crew members also convey they have finished shooting Aloha Heart.

On May 22, 2023, Reel News Hawaii shared Aloha Heart wrapped a 3-week shoot on Oahu.

The day before this, Hawaii-based Cinematographer Ruben Carrillo posted the beautiful view of Kualoa Ranch, and he added it's the last day of shooting for Hallmark's Aloha Heart movie.

The cast of Aloha Heart Sasha Dominy shared some glimpses while waiting to board her flight back to NYC from Oahu, Hawaii.

Dominy added they shot for three weeks in her home state, Oahu, Hawaii. As a result, she could reconnect with her old friends and had a hike over her hometown, Kailua, in Hawaii.

The actress added that the past month has been a literal whirlwind and as cliche as it sounds.

While working on the set, she made a new friend and feels the crew is legendary.

Besides this, some other hit Hallmark movies has shot in Hawaii, including You Had Me at Aloha, Groundswell, Two Tickets to Paraside, and Hidden Gems.
