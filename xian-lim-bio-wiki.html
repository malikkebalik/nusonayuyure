Xian Lim Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Who is Xian Lim?

Xian Lim is a Filipino actor, singer, professional basketball player, and model. People mostly know him for his roles in ‘My Binondo Girl, ‘Bakit Hindi Ka Crush ng Crush Mo?’, ‘Everything About Her, and ‘The Reunion’ among others.

Who are the parents of Xian Lim?

Xian Lim was born in San Francisco, California on July 12, 1989, to parents Godofredo Lim-Uy and Mary Anne Cruz Lim. He moved to Manila, Philippines six months after his birth along with his family. Additionally, he was raised by his mother who is a former model turned piano teacher. He became interested in the world of music and acting in his childhood years. He is of American and Philippine nationality. Furthermore, there are no details available about his ethnicity at present.

Talking about his education, Lim attended Skyline College in San Bruno, California briefly, and studied Psychology. Later, he attended college as a basketball varsity scholar at the University of the East in Manila.

Xian Lim’s Career, Salary, Net Worth

Lim initially became involved in modeling, Next, he had a few minor roles in several projects. He appeared as Rick Alejandro in the TV series ‘My Only Hope’. In 2009, he had a role in another TV series ‘Katorse’. Additionally, he also played Jake in ‘Midnight DJ’. He also had a role as Luis in another TV series ‘Rubi’ in 2010. Since then, he has appeared in numerous other movies and television series. All in all, he has over 25 credits as an actor.

Some other movies and television series that Lim has appeared in are ‘Sin Island’, ‘Maalaala mo kaya’, ‘Dear Other Self’, ‘A Love to Last, ‘The Story of Us’, ‘Everything About Her’, ‘All You Need Is Pag-big, ‘Must Date the Playboy’, ‘Past Tense’, ‘Bride for Rent, ‘Bakit Hindi ka crush ng crush mo?’, ‘Toda Max’, ‘Ina, Kapatid, Anak, ‘My Cactus Heart’, ‘My Binondo Girl’, ‘100 Days to Heaven’, ‘Minsan lang Kita iibigin’, and ‘Mamarazzi’ among others. In addition, Lim signed a contract with Viva Artists Agency in January 2018. Furthermore, he is also involved in various charity games. For the 2018-2019 season, he will also play for the Mandaluyong El Tigre in MPBL.

Xian Lim garnered the FAMAS Award nomination in 2014 and also won the award in 2013. Additionally, he has also won the Special Award in Star Awards for Movies. All in all, he has 3 wins and 5 nominations to his name to date.

Lim has not revealed his current salary. Furthermore, there are no details available relating to his estimated net worth.
